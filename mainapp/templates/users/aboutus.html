Our Story


The restaurant Drizzy, first opened in 2081 in “Kalanki”, one of the oldest cities of Kathmandu. In our first year, the restaurant was awarded its first Michelin star and has retained it since.

The same year, the restaurant relocated to the 4th floor of the Kathmandu Bernhardt College in Bafal and was recently redesigned by award-winning architecture expert Sudip Bohara.In warmer months of the year the restaurant moves to the 5th floor of the building, offering a unique outdoor setting with panoramic view of the historical temple Swayambhu and the beautiful hills.
